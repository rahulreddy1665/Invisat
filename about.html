About us.

Welcome toInvisat Pharmaceuticals

At Invisat Pharmaceuticals, we believe quality medicines should be accessible and affordable. Since starting up in 2000 with just a small team of passionate marketers, we've blossomed into a leading pharmaceutical and nutraceutical manufacturer providing quality products across South India.
Headquartered in a state-of-the-art facility, our 100-strong workforce now creates trusted prescription drugs and health supplements for physicians, pharmacies, and international distributors. Particular about safety, we comply with the industry’s most stringent certifications, audits, and guidelines.
To ensure high quality, we maintain certifications in ISO 9001, ISO 22000, HACCP, Halal, WHO GMP, GHP, and US FDA standards. By following strict quality processes and building relationships in the medical community, we aim to excel at producing medications and supplements that improve health.
We are committed to growth while retaining a collaborative company culture. We continue building an organization that brings value to customers through reliable service and safe, effective products. Even as we evolve, our focus remains on scientific innovation aimed at affordably enhancing quality of life

MiSSION

To blend innovation with affordability, ensuring our pharmaceutical and nutraceutical solutions empower people to lead healthier lives, while adhering to the highest global quality standards.

VISION

To continuously push the boundaries of pharmaceutical excellence, shaping a future where affordable, safe, and effective healthcare solutions are available to everyone, everywhere.

Our Founders

Our founders S. Rajasekar Baba and V. Manjula Rajasekar Baba established Invisat Pharmaceuticals in 2000 with a vision to make quality medicines accessible and affordable. Their commitment to scientific innovation and customer value guides us two decades later. Rajasekar brought pharmaceutical expertise and an entrepreneurial spirit. Manjula lent crucial marketing insight and a passion for community service.
Together they built a company culture focused on quality, reliability and care. We carry their dedication forward as we continue expanding our facilities and product lines to help more people through science and service. Guided by their principles, we constantly strive to enhance lives by delivering trusted pharmaceuticals and nutraceuticals
